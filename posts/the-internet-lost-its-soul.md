The web used to be a place full of personality, quirkiness, and fun. I would browse websites on groceries for hours. Stumble across someone’s final fantasy 7 fan site, covered in weird animated gifs and playing a song from the game. 






# The internet had wonder. Intrigue. Personality. Soul. 







Now everyone is trapped in a boring profile. 
Our creations are stuck in a grid. 
Instead of exploring we just slurp down a feed.






We’ve lost the beauty of the open web.
Where everyone had a home. 
Where everyone was welcome. 
Where we created for ourselves. 
Not for views or likes. 
**Just for the joy of it.**
