# Start Here

I spent 20 years building a career in tech. Jobs today reward people who think, to analyze, to optimize. But that made me miserable.

I wanted to make something meaningful. Something that moves people. I wanted to feel, not think. To be in touch with my body.

I’ve made changes big (moving to Bali) and small (choosing animated wallpapers that inspire me) that made a huge difference in my happiness. I feel more balanced and more alive.

I’m still early in this journey but I want to share what worked for me (and all the things that didn’t 🤦‍♂️ ).

I’m creating blog posts and videos to share everything I’ve learned.

## What I explore

* How to tap into your creativity
* How to listen to your body
* How to find balance
* How to believe in yourself

## Who this is for

* You work in tech and aren’t sure the industry is for you any more.
* You are always in your head and constantly overthinking.
* You crave a simpler life that moves more slowly.
* You don’t want to move another metric, you want to create something that matters.
